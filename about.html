About

Prior to this role, I worked at AIX Investment Group in Burj Khalifa, Dubai, further enriching my experience in offensive security operations. In addition to my professional endeavors, I am passionate about sharing my knowledge and expertise with the cybersecurity community. Through blog posts, workshops, and speaking engagements, I aim to empower aspiring cybersecurity professionals and contribute to the advancement of the field.
